What is devOps

A set of practices that automates the processes between software development and operation teams

Aim to shorten the systems development life cycle while delivering features, fixes, and updates frequently in close alignment with business objectives

devOps model

theory of devOps

Development and operations teams are going to work together across the entire application lifecycle

Benefits

  • Speed - release even faster
  • Rapid Delivery - tight connection with customers
  • Reliability - quality guaranteed by auto-process
  • Scale - robust infrastructure
  • Improved Collaboration - no "wall" between different teams

How we achieve

  • Cultural philosophy
  • Toolchains buildup

Cultural philosophy

Try to break barriers between different teams, there should no need for specific operations team, every engineer can thinking in ops as well.

regardless of their organizational structure, have teams that view the entire development and infrastructure lifecycle as part of their responsibilities
